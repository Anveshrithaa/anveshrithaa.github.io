Mentoring Experience
AthenaAI - Mentoring female undergraduate students

Who is it for?

  • I am managing a small self-formed student group through which I am mentoring female freshmen and juniors along with some of my female peers who are interested in Computer Science, especially in machine learning and Artificial Intelligence, through constant networking for peer learning, sharing ideas and opportunities in this dynamic field to keep each other motivated.

What is it for?

  • I have guided around 16 female undergraduate students by sharing research and project ideas, connecting them with students with similar interests to work on collaborative projects, sharing research opportunities within and outside our university, helping them secure research internships, encouraging participation in hackathons and setting weekly targets and practice sessions for competetive coding.

Outcome

  • 5 sophomores and juniors got research internships in the domain of machine learning at premier educational institutes in India like IIT-Madras, IIIT Hyderabad and IIT-BHU.
  • 3 students are currently doing research in NLP and Information Retrieval under a faculty in the CS department at VIT.
  • Many students have participated and won various hackathons and coding events.

If you are an aspiring young woman with the motivation to get the ball rolling, do reach out to me. I'll be glad to connect with you!
